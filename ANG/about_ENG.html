Who am I?

I was born in 2003. I studied computer science in Technikum Łączności in Kraków. Now, im studying Film and Television Directing in AMA Film Academy. David Lynch said, that looking for ideas is like fishing. If that's so, im a full time fisherman. Every second I'm looking for a new ideas. I make films, write scripts, short stories, and text games.


My experience.
