Closer - Cinematic

Closer - Unreal Engine cinematic

To create this short post-apocalyptic story, I began with research and writing a concise script. I then designed and built the entire environment, including level art and lighting. After that, I worked on animations — retargeting and fine-tuning body movements, while facial animations were created partly by hand and partly recorded using Live Link. Finally, I focused on camera work, from blocking to dynamic movement. The whole project was executed in Unreal Engine 5.
